        What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?
        
      
        GEO means optimizing your website so AI assistants and AI-powered search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, etc.) can find, understand, and fairly represent your content. It overlaps with technical SEO but adds focus on AI crawler policies (robots.txt), machine-readable context (schema, clear headings), and files like llms.txt that tell AI systems how to use your site.
        
      
        Why run a GEO audit?
        
      
          
        
        • See if major AI bots are blocked from crawling your public pages.
          • 
        
        • Check whether structured data helps engines summarize your brand accurately.
          • 
        
        • Spot missing titles, descriptions, or H1s that weaken how your site is described in AI answers.
          • 
        
        • Know if you publish an llms.txt for transparent AI-use policies.
